Calligraphy

At age 10, I wrote calligraphy for the Mid-Autumn Festival celebration at school.

When I was 14, I taught my younger brother how to write Spring Festival couplets at home.

During middle school, I wrote Spring Festival couplets at the school's Wenyuan Pavilion and gave them to teachers.

In high school, I wrote Spring Festival couplets at home.

During the summer break of my second year in high school, I worked as a teaching assistant for my calligraphy teacher.

My handwritten Spring Festival couplets were displayed at the entrance of my classroom at school.

I wrote the character "Fu" (meaning fortune or blessing) in the Wenyuan Pavilion.
