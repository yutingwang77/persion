Welcome to my world before 17! I'm Yuting (Joana) Wang.

你好呀！我是王钰廷，来自杭州。

I come from the picturesque Hangzhou Xuejun Wenyuan Middle School. As someone captivated by aesthetics, I find joy in diverse forms of beauty: from the charm of my school grounds to the allure of intricate patterns, the elegance of well-crafted calligraphy works, the artistry of nail designs, the grandeur of Peking Opera stages, the sophistication of black paper fans, the sleek design of boxing gloves, and the grace of punching action.

If you share my appreciation for life's visual wonders, feel free to explore the navigation bar above. Through these images and words, I hope to invite you into a world where beauty flourishes in unexpected places :)
