Painting

Title: "Four Seasons of Fortune and Longevity - Bird and Flower Paintings"

Size: 69cm x 46cm (27.2" x 18.1")

Style: Traditional Chinese Painting

Theme: This series depicts pomegranates, magpies, mandarin ducks, azure-winged magpies, and peach blossoms, embodying natural harmony and cultural symbolism. Each element represents auspicious meanings in Chinese tradition: fertility, joy, love, good fortune, and longevity. The paintings skillfully blend artistic beauty with cultural wishes for a prosperous and harmonious life.

Title：Echoes of Orchards in Tea

Style: Chinese Brush Painting on Porcelain

Theme：This elegant tea set showcases the perfect blend of traditional Chinese painting and ceramic art. Each teacup is meticulously painted with fruits from different seasons: loquats in spring, grapes in fall, and peaches or apricots in summer. These fruits not only symbolize the change of seasons, but also represent abundance, prosperity and vitality. This tea set is not only a practical tea drinking vessel, but also a piece of art that blends tradition and modernity.
