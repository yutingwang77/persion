Power and Grace: My Peking Opera Role as Mu Guiying

During the summer of 2023, I filmed a promotional video for Chinese opera in my hometown, Wuyi I chose the character of Mu Guiying because she defies traditional stereotypes about women. Mu Guiying is a heroine who embodies the resilience and courage to fight for her country. Through Mu Guiying, I aim not only to showcase the rich cultural heritage of Chinese opera to the world but also to convey the diversity of roles, emphasizing that no character should be confined by gender, birth, or societal expectations!
